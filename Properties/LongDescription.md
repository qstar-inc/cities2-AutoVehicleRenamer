# Auto Vehicle Renamer
Automatically renames vehicles to include their origin information on spawn.

## Features
* Renames vehicles from buildings with custom names or from outside connections
* Optional renaming of vehicles from buildings with default names
* Customizable separator
* Selectable formatting

## What does it rename?
#### Service Vehicles:
* Agent Car
* Ambulance
* Delivery Trucks
* Evacuation Bus
* Fire Engine
* Firefighting Helicopter
* Garbage Truck
* Medical Helicopter
* Park Maintenance Vehicle
* Police Car
* Police Helicopter
* Post Van
* Prison Van
* Snowplow
#### Public Transport &amp; Cargo
* Aircrafts
* Bus
* Subways
* Taxi
* Trains
* Trams
* Watercrafts

### What does it not rename?
* Personal Vehicles
* Specialized Industry Vehicles

## DISCLAIMER
* Safe to add to existing save. (Button in Options can be used to rename all vehicles on the map)
* Safe to remove anytime. (Existing renames will exist until the vehicle despawns)
